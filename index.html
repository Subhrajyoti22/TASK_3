About Us

Set along the Bay of Bengal, this casual seaside hotel is a 3-minute walk from Tajpur Beach, 7 km from Sankarpur Fishing Harbour and 11 km from Badalpur Train Station. The low-key rooms feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, living areas, sofas and en suite bathrooms. They also have shared terraces. Parking is free. Other amenities include a restaurant, and a garden with swings and gazebos. There's also a private path to the beach.
